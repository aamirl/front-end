How Does it Work?

For Buyers
  1. Search through the listings for the perfect item you've been looking for.
  2. Message the seller with any questions or concerns or make an offer.
  3. Reserve the item and pay for it in advance. This way you won't need to bring cash with you to the meetup. This is not a transaction, no money is being moved at this time. The transaction is completed at the time of meetup only when you are satisfied with the product and you transfer the money to the seller right in the app. You can cancel your reserved item for any reason.
  4. Enjoy your new product!
For Sellers
  1. Create a listing through your Sellyx Account.
  2. Receive offers or reservations.
  3. Broadcast live demos of the item.
  4. Meetup with the buyer and have them pay you directly in the app once they are satisfied with the product.

SellyxPay

SellyxPay is our in-house banking system that allows users to upload money, receive money, and also make purchases.

Upload

Uploading money is simple. You can use your debit/credit card or link a bank account. There are no fees when uploading money to SellyxPay.

Purchase

Use your SellyxPay balance to purchase items. It's the safe, simple, and secure way to buy on Sellyx.

Withdraw/Transfer

Money is deposited into your SellyxPay account when you successfully sell an item. You can use that balance to make purchases, withdraw the money to your personal bank account, or transfer to friends or family on Sellyx. We take a small 3.8% fee when withdrawing to your bank.

Why Use Sellyx?

Safety

Our in app purchasing feature allows you to buy items before meeting up so you have the peace of mind knowing you won't need to bring cash with you. Complete the transaction with just the press of a button once you are satisfied with the item.

Live Video Demos

With Sellyx, you can request live video demos from the comfort of your phone. Meet the seller, see the product and ask questions before you finalize the purchase or make the drive.

Trust

Buy and sell from trusted users. You can pick and choose who you want to deal with based on their reviews.

No Listing or Membership Fees

Absolutely no listing or membership fees. We do charge buyers a 3% transaction fee on purchases made with a credit/debit card and withdrawl fees of 3.8% to sellers. There are no fees when purchasing with Sellyxpay.

Anyone Can Easily Sell

Whether you are a first-time seller or a retail company with thousands of locations, selling locally has never been easier. Easily get verified, add new or existing inventory and begin selling within a few minutes.

Buy Locally

Ever wonder what's being sold around you? Using Sellyx, people and stores can post listings that you can browse and buy. Sort by distance, negotiate a price and even pay for the item in advance if you want. Then simply go pick it up.
